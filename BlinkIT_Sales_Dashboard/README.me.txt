Project Title: BlinkIT Analysis

Description:This dashboard is aimed at helping stakeholders understand Blinkit’s performance across various dimensions including:


Outlet Location Types


Outlet Sizes (Small, Medium, High)


Item Categories


Sales & Revenue Trends


Outlet Establishment Trends Over Time


Customer Ratings & Feedback


Fat Content Trends by Outlet Tier

How to use:Open the .pbix file in Power BI Desktop.


Replace or update the data source if needed.


Interact with filters to explore sales patterns.
Use insights to make data-driven business decisions.
